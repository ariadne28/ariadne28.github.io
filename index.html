Digital Humanist, Writer, Content Creator

Bio

Currently a student of master's degree programme in Language Technologies and Digital Humanities at the University of Turin, I previously obtained a bachelor's degree in Asian Languages, Markets and Cultures at the University of Bologna. When I'm not studying or working, I'm usually reading a book, writing novels or short stories (or at least trying to do so), fumbling with code or cuddling my cat. I am keen on book editing and publishing, languages and translation. You might also find me babbling about books and other nerd stuff on Instagram (currently on: @_thelastwriter). Communication, in all its forms, is my secret superpower.

My main interests revolve around the areas of gender and queer studies, paper and digital publishing, digital humanities, languages and cultures, pop culture and lots of other things which might appear or disappear from this page quite often.

While being enthusiastic about the implementation of digital technolgies for research and innovation, I oppose to the use of generative AI in creative projects (novels, movie scripts, visual arts, etc.) when driven by profit, and I strongly advocate for the protection and respect of human artistic expression. My goal is to foster innovation and accessibility in the digital domain, while actively safeguarding the value of inherently human creativity, which I believe is the only force capable of giving culture both a meaning and a purpose.
